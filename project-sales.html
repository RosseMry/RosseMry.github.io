Overview

This dashboard was built to answer a core business question: where are sales growing, and where are they declining? Using an open source dataset simulating a multi-manufacturer retail environment, I designed a reporting tool that gives a complete view of commercial performance at a glance.

What it does

The dashboard is structured around three layers of analysis. The main view gives an executive summary — total revenue, product count, and manufacturer breakdown at a glance. The trend view compares current month performance against the previous month using DAX time intelligence functions, making it easy to spot seasonal patterns. The competitive view uses a treemap to visualize market share by manufacturer.

All visuals are connected through dynamic filters by country, manufacturer, product, and date range — so any team member can slice the data to answer their specific question without touching the underlying model.

Key insight

Analyzing monthly trends revealed that May consistently outperforms all other months — a pattern that becomes visible only when looking at multi-year aggregated data. This kind of insight is invisible in raw spreadsheets but immediately clear in a well-structured BI report.
